## What this is

Image generation is the art studio: create new images or edit existing ones.

## Great for

- marketing mockups
- concept art
- “show me three options” brainstorming

<Callout kind="note" title="Good prompts are design briefs">
Subject, style, constraints, and what must not appear. That’s it.
</Callout>

## Safety basics

- clarify whether user uploads can be stored
- avoid generating deceptive content
- keep brand usage rules clear (logos, trademarks, etc.)
